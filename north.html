North Zone Beaches

Discover the rugged beauty and pristine shores of the North.

Reynisfjara Beach, Iceland

Vik, Iceland Best time: Jun - Aug

Famous for its black sand, basalt columns, and powerful Atlantic waves. A truly unique and dramatic landscape.

Travel Tips:

Be careful of "sneaker waves" which can be dangerous. Stay well back from the water's edge.

Lofoten Islands Beaches, Norway

Nordland, Norway Best time: May - Sep

Arctic beaches with crystal clear turquoise water and white sand, surrounded by dramatic mountains.

Travel Tips:

Bring warm clothing even in summer. Great for surfing and midnight sun photography.

Cannon Beach, USA

Oregon, USA Best time: Jul - Sep

Iconic for Haystack Rock, this vast sandy beach offers stunning sunsets and tide pools.

Travel Tips:

Explore the tide pools at low tide to see starfish and anemones.

Durness Beach, Scotland

Sutherland, Scotland Best time: May - Aug

A spectacular coastline with rocky cliffs, dunes, and turquoise waters that rival the Caribbean.

Travel Tips:

Visit Smoo Cave nearby for a unique geological experience.

Long Beach, Canada

Tofino, BC, Canada Best time: Jun - Sep

Located in Pacific Rim National Park Reserve, it's the longest sand beach on Vancouver Island.

Travel Tips:

Popular for storm watching in winter and surfing year-round.
