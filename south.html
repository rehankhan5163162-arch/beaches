South Zone Beaches

Experience the warmth and vibrancy of the Southern Hemisphere.

Whitehaven Beach, Australia

Whitsunday Island, Australia Best time: Sep - Nov

Famous for its crystal white silica sands and turquoise waters. It's often voted the world's best beach.

Travel Tips:

Take a seaplane tour for the iconic view of Hill Inlet.

Bora Bora, French Polynesia

French Polynesia Best time: May - Oct

A romantic paradise featuring overwater bungalows and a stunning lagoon protected by a coral reef.

Travel Tips:

Don't miss snorkeling with sharks and rays in the lagoon.

Anse Source d'Argent, Seychelles

La Digue, Seychelles Best time: Apr - May, Oct - Nov

Renowned for its giant granite boulders, shallow clear waters, and pink sands.

Travel Tips:

Arrive early to avoid the crowds and get the best photos.

Copacabana, Brazil

Rio de Janeiro, Brazil Best time: Dec - Mar

One of the most famous beaches in the world, known for its vibrant atmosphere and 4km stretch of sand.

Travel Tips:

Enjoy a caipirinha at one of the many beach kiosks.

Boulders Beach, South Africa

Cape Town, South Africa Best time: Oct - Mar

Famous for its colony of African Penguins which settle round the beach.

Travel Tips:

Stick to the boardwalks to view the penguins without disturbing them.
