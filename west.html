West Zone Beaches

Chase the sunset across the magnificent Western shores.

Santa Monica Beach, USA

California, USA Best time: May - Sep

Iconic for its pier, amusement park, and vast stretch of sand. A classic Californian experience.

Travel Tips:

Rent a bike and ride along the Marvin Braude Bike Trail.

Tulum Beach, Mexico

Quintana Roo, Mexico Best time: Nov - Dec

Bohemian vibe with ancient Mayan ruins overlooking the Caribbean Sea.

Travel Tips:

Visit the ruins early in the morning to beat the heat and crowds.

Waikiki Beach, USA

Hawaii, USA Best time: Apr - Jun

World-famous for its surfing history and view of Diamond Head crater.

Travel Tips:

Take a surfing lesson in the gentle waves, perfect for beginners.

Manuel Antonio, Costa Rica

Puntarenas, Costa Rica Best time: Dec - Apr

Where the rainforest meets the sea. Spot monkeys and sloths right on the beach.

Travel Tips:

Hire a guide to help you spot wildlife in the national park.

Navagio Beach, Greece

Zakynthos, Greece Best time: May - Oct

Also known as Shipwreck Beach, accessible only by boat and famous for the wreck of MV Panagiotis.

Travel Tips:

View the beach from the cliffs above for the most iconic photo.
