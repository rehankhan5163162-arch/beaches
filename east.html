East Zone Beaches

Where the sun rises over exotic sands and cultures.

Maya Bay, Thailand

Koh Phi Phi, Thailand Best time: Nov - Apr

Made famous by the movie "The Beach", it offers stunning limestone cliffs and clear waters.

Travel Tips:

Check for closure dates as it closes periodically for ecological recovery.

Nusa Dua, Bali

Bali, Indonesia Best time: Apr - Oct

A luxurious beach resort area known for its clean white sands and calm waters.

Travel Tips:

Great for water sports and family vacations due to calm tides.

El Nido, Philippines

Palawan, Philippines Best time: Dec - May

Famous for its dramatic limestone formations and secret lagoons.

Travel Tips:

Take an island-hopping tour to discover hidden beaches.

Radhanagar Beach, India

Havelock Island, India Best time: Oct - May

Often ranked as the best beach in Asia, known for its mesmerizing sunset and white sand.

Travel Tips:

No water sports allowed here, making it perfect for swimming and relaxation.

Maldive Islands

Maldives Best time: Nov - Apr

The epitome of tropical paradise with overwater villas and vibrant coral reefs.

Travel Tips:

Perfect for honeymooners and diving enthusiasts.
